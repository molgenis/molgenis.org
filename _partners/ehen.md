The [European Human Exposome Network](https://www.humanexposome.eu/) combines nine research projects funded under Horizon 2020, the EU Framework Programme for Research and Innovation: EXPANSE / EQUAL-LIFE / LONGITOOLS  ATHLETE / EXIMIOUS / HEDIMED HEAP / REMEDIA / EPHOR. Together, they represent 106 M€ funding from the EU over 5 years. The Human Exposome is the study of how elements we are exposed to via our diet, lifestyle and the environment we live and work in, affect out health. MOLGENIS participated in ATHLETE and LongITools and provided the integrated catalogue for the network.
