Our Variant Interpretation Pipeline (VIP) highlighted by Oxford Nanopore Technologies on their blog "Ending the diagnostic odyssey starts here", posted [here](https://nanoporetech.com/blog/reshaping-rare-disease-diagnosis-an-end-to-the-diagnostic-odyssey).

## Summary
The blog highlights long-read sequencing technology from Oxford Nanopore Technologies as a potential solution.
Unlike standard methods, it reads longer stretches of DNA, giving a more complete view of the genome.
This allows it to detect complex genetic changes and patterns that other methods may overlook.
It can also capture epigenetic information, such as DNA methylation, in the same test.
