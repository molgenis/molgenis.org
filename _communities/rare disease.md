A rare disease is any disease that affects a small percentage of the population. Due to the low prevalence of each disease, medical expertise is rare, 
knowledge is scarce, care offerings inadequate and research limited. With MOLGENIS we aim to help and bring as much data together as we can and provide 
tools to analyse these data to improve diagnosis, treatment, and provide these patients the benefits of research.
